About Us

Welcome to the World of 24 Solar Terms Flavors

Discover a unique culinary journey inspired by the 24 Chinese Solar Terms — a timeless guide to nature’s rhythm, changing seasons, and human wellbeing. Each solar term brings its own seasonal energy, reflected in the colors, ingredients, and flavors of the dishes we create. Seasons flavor meets cuisine — a harmonious blend where tradition, health, and taste come together. Our menus are carefully crafted to honor the essence of each solar term, helping you reconnect with nature through vibrant, nourishing flavors. Explore how ancient wisdom and modern taste unite to create unforgettable seasonal dining experiences.

24 healthiest flavors

Spring Pancakes (春饼)

Thin pancakes filled with stir-fried seasonal veggies (like Chinese chives, bean sprouts). It symbolizes "biting the spring" and refreshes the body after winter.

Price: ¥ 20-40

Shepherd’s Purse Wonton (荠菜馄饨)

Wontons stuffed with minced shepherd’s purse and pork. Light, nutritious, and perfect for strengthening after cold weather.

Price: ¥ 15-35

Green Bean Soup (绿豆汤)

A sweet, cool soup made by boiling mung beans with water and a little sugar. It detoxes, cools down body heat, and relieves summer fatigue.

Price: ¥ 10-20

Bitter Melon Stir-Fry (苦瓜炒蛋)

Sliced bitter melon quickly stir-fried with eggs. A cooling dish that clears heat and boosts the immune system.

Price: ¥ 30-40

Stewed Pear with Rock Sugar (冰糖炖雪梨)

Pears are hollowed out, filled with rock sugar, and steamed until soft. Moistens dry lungs and soothes dry coughs common in autumn.

Price: ¥ 25-45

Lily Bulb and Tremella Soup (百合银耳羹)

A slightly sweet soup made with lily bulbs, white fungus (tremella), and jujube dates. It nourishes yin, boosts skin health, and combats dryness.

Price: ¥ 30-60

Lamb Hotpot (羊肉火锅)

Slices of lamb cooked in a hot, herbal broth with winter vegetables. Very warming, tonifies yang, and strengthens resistance to cold.

Price: ¥ 80-150

Steamed Chinese Yam with Goji Berries (枸杞蒸山药)

Chinese yam slices steamed with goji berries and a bit of honey. Strengthens digestion and boosts energy without feeling heavy.

Price: ¥ 25-50
